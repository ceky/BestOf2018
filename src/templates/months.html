January

I got the chance to visit Dublin. Or better said the pubs in Dublin. Cause that's where I have the best memories.

February

Vladeasa was the craziest running competiton ever. The extreme weather transformed it into something to remeber.

March

Had the opportunity from my work to travel to the office in Porto. The hills are offering the city a stunning view. If you try the Porto wine, the views become even more amazing.

April

Malaga is my second home. Such a pleasure to return there whenever I can. Also, eating at chiringuitos is the best thing ever.

May

There is no doubt that the running competition in Apuseni is my favorite. So grateful to be there for the third time in a row.

June

Quitting smoking is the thing that I'm the most proud of achieving in 2018. Even if I considered myself a social smoker, in June I put an end to this nonsense.

July

Ran my first trail marathon in Cindrel. Also the Rodnei Sky Race competition was one of the best. Running in the clouds and the landscapes from 2000m are wonderful.

August

I might have missed Untold and Electric Castle festivals in Cluj. But hey, Awake festival was such a nice surprise. 10/10 would return there.

September

Zilele Nordului. There must be something that I like about this festival in Moldova since I've been there for the past four years. But as always in life, it's all about the people you surround yourself with.

October

Stepping into the 30s also means you get together with a large group of friends on fewer ocasions than before. A birthday party and a night spent outside the city in Salard was a good reason to reunite.

November

Seeing three of my favorite bands, Robin And The Backstabbers, Byron and The Mono Jacks playing in Cluj with an orchestra was just amazing.

December

It might be close to Cluj, but only now I've been in a city break in Budapest. The food, the Danube cruise and playing an escape room for the first time, made this holiday one of the best.
